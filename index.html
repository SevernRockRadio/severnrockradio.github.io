New Website!

Back in August, I remember my son telling me the benifits of owning an online website. So I told him that if he could produce a profesional website, and present it to me, we would have a chat about it. No later than two days passed before I was shown a demo of the website. It looked nothing like the one we have today, so I told him to go back an review what he had ... made, and come back with a more finished piece.

After a couple of weeks had passed, and a conversation about a better computer to edit a Severn Rock Radio Doccumentary had been discussed, Kieran came back to me with a very well put together website. This version is most likely identical to the one your viewing this blog post on! However, I did have a few critiques about the images which were used within the website but other than that, I loved it!
